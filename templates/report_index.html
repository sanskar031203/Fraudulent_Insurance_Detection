Fraud Detection Insights

Categories of Fraud Detection

1. Inflated Medical Bills

This category identifies claims where medical bills have been artificially increased. Our system calculates the percentage rise in the claimed amount compared to typical expenses for similar cases. If the increase exceeds the threshold of 30%, the claim is flagged as potentially fraudulent.

  • Uses comparative analysis of medical costs for similar treatments.
  • Highlights discrepancies to reduce unjustified payouts.

2. Fake Admission Sheets

Admission sheets are analyzed for suspicious or unnecessary details. The system detects keywords such as "dizziness" combined with unrelated diagnoses, flagging such cases as suspicious. This ensures that claims based on fabricated or exaggerated documents are identified effectively.

  • Uses keyword and pattern detection algorithms.
  • Focuses on eliminating fraudulent admissions to avoid unwarranted claims.
